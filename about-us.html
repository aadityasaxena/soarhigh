SOAR is inspired by The Startup Launchbook:

A Practical Guide for Launching Customer-Centric Ventures. The core framework in the book, The Startup Maturity Model (SMM), contains best-practices that contribute to launching customer-centric startups - these practices are arranged in a staged architecture consisting of Five Maturity Levels, offering a clear and actionable roadmap for startup founders. SMM seamlessly integrates concepts from the domains of Design Thinking, Lean Startups, Strategy, Operations and Human Capital Management.

Startup Launch Book

The SOAR assessment, based on decades of experience with startups worldwide, will take you through Five Maturity Levels so you can pinpoint how far along you are. You will learn which capabilities investors look for at each stage, giving you powerful ideas for your fund-raise activities.

Startup founders will find insights on understanding customers’ needs, analyzing competition, building a strong team, launching successfully and scaling prudently. These best practices have been curated based on our experience, and by analyzing hundreds of startup success and failure lessons worldwide.

Given the multiple priorities screaming for attention in startups, it is quite easy to lose sight of the “big picture” – of building a robust and enduring businesses. SOAR provides for various levels of self-assessment to ensure that startups have a realistic sense of their current status and progress.

No more “shooting in the dark”, or “taking on more than what one can chew on”.  And certainly, no more of the status-quo. We provide a structured roadmap to build an effective startup – with a sharp focus on systems, developing the right culture, gaining market traction and developing talent. The roadmap brings a sense of discipline with adequate flexibility to maneuver a venture-specific journey.
